Official Statement

A Message of Academic Excellence and Cultural Bridge-Building

February 12, 2026

To my esteemed colleagues, dedicated students, and the broader academic community,

It is with profound gratitude and renewed purpose that I address you today. Throughout my tenure at Ain Shams University, I have remained steadfast in my commitment to advancing Hebrew studies, fostering intercultural dialogue, and nurturing the next generation of scholars. Our discipline is not merely about language acquisition—it is about building bridges of understanding between cultures and civilizations.

The study of Hebrew language and literature opens windows into a rich cultural heritage while simultaneously strengthening our own identity and scholarly tradition. I have dedicated my career to demonstrating how linguistic scholarship can serve as a powerful tool for mutual respect, academic excellence, and peaceful coexistence.

To my students: You are the future of this discipline. I see in your enthusiasm and dedication the promise of continued academic achievement. To my colleagues: Your collaborative spirit and intellectual rigor have made our department a beacon of excellence in the region.

As we navigate an increasingly complex global landscape, the role of humanities scholars becomes ever more vital. I pledge to continue my work with renewed vigor—publishing, teaching, and mentoring—always upholding the highest standards of academic integrity and intellectual curiosity.

Dr. Doaa Saif El-Dine
